Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Whole milk
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Cinnamon
  • Brown Sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. mix your dry ingredients together in a big bowl and this includes the flour, sugar, and salt.
  3. warm the milk and butter together, and then whisk in the yeast until it has dissolved.
  4. Pour this mixture over the dry ingredients, add the egg and then mix everything together.
  5. Transfer dough to your work surface and then knead by hand for 3 minutes until a soft dough forms
  6. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes
  7. Roll out the dough and then top with softened butter and the brown sugar & cinnamon mixture.
  8. Roll up the dough and then use your sharpest knife to cut into 10-12 rolls.
  9. Arrange your rolls in a lightly greased 9-inch or 10-inch pan
  10. Bake for 12-15 minutes
